Developer Text

There are two leftover text fragments in the game which are remnants from when the developers added Multitap support to the game. The first half-fragment is found at

0x006B5E  ...

START OF MULTI5 BIOS

...and the second half is found just below:

MODIFIED FROM SHVC MULTI5 BIOS Ver2.00END OF MULTI5 BIOS

The second fragment begins at

0x02F526  ...

START OF MULTI5 CONNECT

and again, ends just below:

MODIFIED FROM SHVC MULTI5 CONNECT CHK Ver1.00END OF MULTI5 CONNECT CHk

(Source:  Original TCRF research )
