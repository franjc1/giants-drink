Debug Mode

Oh, boy...to enable debug mode, do the following sequence:

Press and hold  Up , then  A ; release  Up , then  A .

Press and hold  Right , then  A ; release  Right , then  A .

Press and hold  Down , then  A ; release  Down , then  A .

Press and hold  Left , then  A ; release  Left , then  A .

Press and hold  Left , then  B .

You should hear a sound if you entered the sequence correctly. A stage select option will now replace the Continue option on the title screen; select it and use the D-Pad to select the level. In-game, pause with  Start  and press  Select  to cycle through the weapons.

(Source:  CaH4e3 )
