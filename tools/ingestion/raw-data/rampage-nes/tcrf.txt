Unused Graphics

Pictures of George and Lizzie in human form, which were to appear during the screens that preceded certain levels had they followed the format of the arcade version.

Day Select

Press  B + Select  on Controller 1 and  B  on Controller 2 simultaneously at the title screen. After the mutant selection screen, a screen reading "SECRET COMMAND DAY SELECT" will follow.

Oddly, while the game has 128 levels, only 99 of them can be accessed through this menu.
