Prerelease Info

Unused Music

Like  its Genesis counterpart , the SNES version contains some unused  Turrican II  music. This short song is based on "Alien Squeeze", part of the ambient music that plays during Stage 5-1 of the original game.

As with the other tracks in the SNES version, the instrumentation is extremely rough and the song has some timing issues. This is also the only song that isn't part of the game's music pointer table - index

0E  , which it had probably occupied previously, had been replaced with a null pointer.

The song can be heard in-game using  Pro Action Replay  codes

80CA51EE 80CA5282 80CA5306 7E03340E  .

(Source: KungFuFurby)
