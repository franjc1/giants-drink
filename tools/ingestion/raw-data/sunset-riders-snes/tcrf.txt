Sunset Riders  for the SNES is a very faithful port of the arcade version. Set in the Wild West, play as one of four bounty hunters as you shoot down convicts, outlaws, and the occasional money-grubber.

Extra Lives and Continues

When  1PLAYER ,  2PLAYERS ,  OPTION  is displayed on the title screen, press  R ,  L ,  R ,  L ,  R ,  L ,  A ,  R ,  L ,  R ,  L ,  R ,  L ,  Y . Now enter options.  This sets memory address

7E1FA0

$01  .

You can now select up to  5 REST  and  5 CONTINUE  for any difficulty mode.  Normally,  HARD  is limited to 3 each, and  NORMAL  4 each.
