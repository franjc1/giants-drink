The Tick: The Game, based on The Tick: The Animated Series, and The Tick: The Comic.

SPOOOOOOON!!

Level Select

Set the following at the options menu:

Lives -  7

Continues -  4

Arthurs -  2

Now, go to "Test Sound" and play "Teleport" to make "CHEAT" appear all over on the background. During the game, pause and press  Select  for the Level Select menu. All levels will be sorted by "Story". You can also press  L + R during gameplay to skip the current level.

(Source: GameFAQs)
