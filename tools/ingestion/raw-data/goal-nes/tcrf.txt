Regional Differences

Title Screen

Besides the redone logo, the background was changed to black and as a result the player was recolored to not blend in. The European version just updates the copyright info.

Players

All of the players received some touch-ups. In addition, the player names were changed; Echenkaiser (エッヘンカイザー), Pelé (パレ), and Uma Kamado (カマドウマ) were changed to Hansen, Roko, and Juarez, respectively.
