Back to the Future Part II & III

sounds cool in theory.

For the  Part II  portion, Biff has taken various items from 1955, 1985, and 2015 and spread them all over the place in those time periods; you, as Marty, need to find the items and return them to their proper places in time. The problem is that you have 30 items to find and three years to return them to their time periods, without the help of maps or passwords. As you can imagine, this can make the game that much more frustrating.

The  Part III  portion, on the other hand, is confined to one time period (1885) with fewer items and a smaller map, making things a bit simpler.
