The NES

Lode Runner

is a port of the computer game of the same name, which was turned into a sequel to

Bomberman  .

Select Secrets

While playing, press Select to view current level, then press the following buttons for different effects:

Level Select: 
Press  B  to go up a level or press  A  to go down a level, then  Start  to play the stage.

Character Speed: 
Hold Select and press  A  (up to 50 times) to increase the game's speed or  B  to lower it.

Regional Differences

Famicom

NES

The NES version adds "BY BRODERBUND SOFTWARE INC" below the Lode Runner logo and removes the brick graphics surrounding the options. In addition to the usual Nintendo of America mention being added to the copyright text,  Lode Runner  creator Doug Smith is now credited, as he was in the original Apple II version.
