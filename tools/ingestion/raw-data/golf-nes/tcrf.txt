Golf

is an early Famicom/NES sports title that features Mario and is known for being programmed by the late Satoru Iwata. A copy of this game would be included in the  Nintendo Switch  firmware until it was removed in v4.0.0.

An unused object sprite for one of the holes.

Regional Differences

Japan/Europe

US

The Japanese and European versions measures distance in meters, the US version was modified to measure in yards instead. The version used in the  Nintendo Switch  firmware is meters for all regions.

(Source: Bro3256)
