Utilities

ESE2 is another popular level editor, working with Sonic 1, Sonic 2, Sonic 2 beta, and Sonic 3k, its tools include, Layout editor, Pallet editor (very good one), Object editor, tile editor, and Playlist Editor (Music Editor). I personally use this as my main editor, but yet SonED does have some special quality's as in seeing the sky in layout editor, and other useful things, if these two apps are used properly together they can churn out some pretty good maps!
