Unused Options

Pro Action Replay code

7F045B04

adds three additional selections to the options menu (highlight Control and hold  Down+Right  to reveal them): Joystick, Sound, and Movie (the last of which is cut off). None of these work, and Joystick having a "6 Button" option may indicate these are leftovers from  the Genesis version .
