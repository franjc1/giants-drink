Rockin' Kats

is a goofy platformer in which Willy, a rockin' kat with a punch gun, must rescue his girlfriend from Mugsy, a stinkin' dog with lots of friends.

Bat

Unused flapping tiles for the Bat enemy, which seem to suggest he was able to fly (and hang sideways?) at one point. In the final game, he just hangs upside down and throws mini-bats at you.
