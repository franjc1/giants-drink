Easter Egg

After completing the game, at the Punisher's final line ("WELL, MY JOB'S FINISHED..."), hold  Up + B + Select  on Controller 1 to have him make a weird, exaggerated, and downright  creepy  face. This was drawn by Holger Liebnitz, one of the game's graphic designers.
