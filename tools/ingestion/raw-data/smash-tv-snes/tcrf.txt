Level Select

Press  Right, Right, Up, Down, R, L  at the player select screen, then start the game. This menu contains options to start at the beginning of each level or at the bosses, plus the credits.

(Source: GameFAQs)

Sound Test

At the player select screen, press  L, R, L, L, R  and listen for a "Bingo!", then start the game.

(Source: GameFAQs)

Hidden Developer Credits

At the Beam Software logo screen, hold  L + R  and press  B  to reveal credits for programmers Jamie Rivett and Graeme Scott, and artist Martin Thompson.

Turbo Mode Test Screen

After the logo screens, hold  L + A  and press  Reset  to see the turbo mode activation animation.

Regional Differences

The Japanese version adds a logo screen for ASCII Corporation before the Acclaim logo. It has the same gradient animation on the top and bottom as the Acclaim logo in the English version, and the Acclaim logo shows with the blue gradients already in place.
