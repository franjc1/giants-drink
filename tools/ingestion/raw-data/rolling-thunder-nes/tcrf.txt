Regional Differences

Japan

US

In the Tengen release the title logo was moved down slightly to accommodate for the "Tengen Presents" text. In addition, the Namco logo was removed from the new game menu.

Sound

In Japan,  Rolling Thunder  uses the Namco 163 mapper (which adds extra sound channels, allowing for fuller sounding music and sound effects), while in the US release Tengen opted to have the game converted to run on its usual Tengen mapper. As such, the sound in the Tengen release had to be redone to compensate for the loss of sound channels.

The names for the songs are taken from the  Game Sound Museum - Namcot-hen Vol 10 - Rolling Thunder  mini-CD album.
