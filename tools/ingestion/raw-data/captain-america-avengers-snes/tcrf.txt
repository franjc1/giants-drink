Development Credits

Located at

0x26

is a development credit text string for the Conversion Programmer, Lennard Feddersen. The full name appears in the game's staff roll.

LEN FEDD

Much later, at

0x18000  , is a development credit related text string for the sound driver programmer.

.v...._9.AUDIO DRIVER (C) 1993 DAVID WARHOL ALL RIGHTS RESERVED
