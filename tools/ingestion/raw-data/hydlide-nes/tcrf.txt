Regional Differences

Title Screen

Japan

North America

In Japan, the game was titled  Hydlide Special .

The  PUSH BUTTON&#160;!  text was moved up in the North American version for more copyright text. Yay.

The  TOSHIBA EMI  credit was removed.

The  T&ESOFT  credit was changed to  T&ESOFT INC , and moved a few blocks to the left.

The North American version actually adds a  ™  symbol that was not present in the Japanese version.

Password

The Japanese version uses 14 characters while the North American version uses 16 characters. Also, the word  Password  was changed to  Input  in the password entry field.
