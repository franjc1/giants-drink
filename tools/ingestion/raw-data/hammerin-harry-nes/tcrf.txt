Regional Differences

Irem Logo

The Japanese version displays the Irem logo before the title screen appears. Strangely enough, this was removed for the European release.

Title Screen

Japan

Europe

The logo was overhauled, the banner was shortened a bit and recolored from orange to dark blue. Two orange borders were also added.

Powerups

Japan

Europe

The bottle of sake was changed to a hot pepper like in the arcade version, while the rice ball (onigiri) was changed to cheese.

Voice Samples
