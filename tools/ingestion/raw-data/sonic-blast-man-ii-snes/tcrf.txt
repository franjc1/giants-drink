Unused Text

Among the text for the options menu (located at

0x2DBB6  ) is

SOUND TEST  ---

However, the code that makes this option available is no longer available in either the Japanese or North American versions of the game.

Sound Driver Credits

A sound driver credit is present at

0x150CD5  .

== Ver1.27 Copyright K.Suemura ==

Regional Differences

Sonic Blast Man's Japanese line from the Japanese version was dubbed into "I FIGHT FOR FREEDOM!" in the North American version.

One of the bosses for Stage 4 is a unmasked female with long hair in the Japanese version, but is changed to a masked male with short hair in the North American version.
