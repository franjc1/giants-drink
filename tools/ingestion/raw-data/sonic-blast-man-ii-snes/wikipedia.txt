Sonic Blast Man II

&#91; b &#93;

is a  beat 'em up  video game developed and published by  Taito  for the  Super Nintendo Entertainment System  in 1994.

Gameplay

[

edit

]

Sonic Blast Man II  is a sequel to the arcade game

Sonic Blast Man  . More gameplay options are added, including new playable characters "Sonia" and "Captain Choyear", and two-player modes.
