Bebe's Kids

is a side-scrolling beat 'em up video game developed by  Radical Entertainment  and published by  Motown Games / Paramount Interactive  for the  Super Nintendo Entertainment System , released in  1994 . It is based on the  1992 animated film of the same name  and, like the film, received largely unfavorable critical reception.

Gameplay

[

edit

]

The player fighting against two mascots.

The player controls either LaShawn or Kahlil as they wreak havoc in Fun World, the  Disneyland -like  theme park  featured in the film.

&#91; 4 &#93;

Similar to both  Konami 's

The Simpsons

and  Kaneko 's

B. Rap Boys  , the player must contend with security guards, disgruntled  mascots , and  pirates  who work for the theme park.

Reception

[

edit

]

Nintendo Power

gave the game a very negative review. While noting that it had "nice graphics and sound", the magazine criticized its "extremely slow action", stating enemies "take huge amounts of damage so battles seem endless", as well as its "poor play control."

&#91; 6 &#93;

In 1997,  Nintendo Power  ranked it the worst video game of all time.

&#91; 7 &#93;

Time Extension  also listed  Bebe's Kids  as one of the worst SNES games.

&#91; 8 &#93;
