Flying Warriors

is a localized remake of

Hiryuu no Ken II

that employs the graphical and mechanical improvements from

Hiryuu no Ken III  , alongside an altered soundtrack including a few tunes from III. It redesigned the transformed states of the main heroes in order to give them Americanized superhero-like designs (or at least what passed for American superheroes in the minds of the Japanese developers), going as far as to even replace the original title theme with a new one that totally doesn't sound like John Williams'  Superman  theme in any form.

The Flying Warriors, mainly Rick, would go on to be used in future Hiryuu no Ken localizations such as  Ultimate Fighter , and in-place of the Hiryuu no Ken characters in cameo appearances in other Culture Brain games (including  Little Ninja Brothers  which was released in the US the same month) up until  Flying Dragon  on Nintendo 64.
It even got a promotional comic in GamePro, which lasted for 12 surprisingly lengthy issues.

Prototype Info

Debug Stage Select

Game Genie  code

GESLAPEI

will re-enable an in-game stage select routine. During the game, hold  A  and press  Start . A number will appear instead of the game's menu. Select a location number, then press  Select .

Debug Passwords

Sound Test

Enter the password  MUSIC . Press  Up / Down  to select a track, and  A  to play it. Press  B  to return to the password screen.

Level Select

Enter the password  8 xy  , where  x  is the stage number from 1-4 and  y  is the sub-stage number from 1-3 (i.e., the password to the last level is  843 ).

View Ending

Enter the password  END  to view the ending sequence.

Character Bios

Enter the password  888  to view the character bios. Press  Select  to return to the game.
