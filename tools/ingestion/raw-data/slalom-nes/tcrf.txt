Slalom

is an early NES game and part of the Sports Series, developed in the UK by a little up-and-coming company called Rare.

Notably,  Slalom  was the first NES game developed by a Western company. It was also the next-to-last Nintendo-published NES game with the original American box layout; the last,  Rad Racer , was released in October.

Developer Credits
