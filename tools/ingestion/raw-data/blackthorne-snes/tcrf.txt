Debugging Functions

This needs some investigation.

Discuss ideas and findings on the  talk page .

Specifically:

Figure out the content of the rightmost entry in the debug display.

See if there's a way to enable these in the US and EU versions.

Interestingly, the Japanese version has multiple debugging functions enabled by default. Just press the corresponding button/button combination during gameplay to access them.

Debug Display

Press  X  on Controller 2. From left to right, the displays show Kyle's X position, Kyle's Y position, the ID of the current level, and an unknown value.

Free-Movement Mode

Press  X + Y + L + R  on Controller 1. Move Kyle around with the  D-Pad , and press  B  to place him.

Power Up Gun

Press  Y  on Controller 2. Repeat up to three times to fully power it up.

Speed Up Movement

Hold  B  on Controller 2. This speeds up the movement of not only Kyle, but also all other characters.

Soft Reset

Press  A + B + L + R  on Controller 1.

(Source:  super-famicom.jp )
