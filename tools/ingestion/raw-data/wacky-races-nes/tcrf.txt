Regional Differences

Title Screen

The background on the title screen was changed from blue to red for the international release. The original Japanese title screen reflects its name in that region: チキチキマシン猛レース (Chiki Chiki Machine Ferocious Race), and it was altered to Wacky Races for the international release.

Interestingly, the US version still retains the theme song from the Japanese cartoon for its title and ending music. Somebody at Atlus probably wasn't paying much attention in that department.
