Regional Differences

Unused Graphics

Alternate fuel/weapon meter parts, loaded into the other CHR-RAM bank and not animated like the ones that are used. Note that the entire number font is also loaded into this bank but actually overwritten.

Preludon Map

Normally, the earliest the player can obtain a Mapping Device is as of Planet 2, Mexomorf. However, a map of Planet 1, Preludon, exists and can be accessed by using a password that gives a Mapping Device early (such as  KBBKBBDBDGBB ).

Since the entire planet is roughly the size of 25% of the map screen, it's a little buggy and displays twice horizontally, but the map is otherwise accurate. It even shows all the items and wormholes (including the one for the optional bonus stage) if the player has a Super Mapping Device.

Unused Planet

A 14th planet, "Dryn Kup Leez", was apparently planned at one point. Enter the Game Genie code

IASSTU

or the password  KBHNBBDBKLBB  to see it. Unfortunately, no data exists for the planet, so the player will spawn in Preludon's Golden Warpship Part area and instantly warp to the Cyber Zone.

Regional Differences

The European version adds this additional screen after the initial copyrights screen, featuring the name of Rare's predecessor who also distributed the first two games in the  Jetman  series.
