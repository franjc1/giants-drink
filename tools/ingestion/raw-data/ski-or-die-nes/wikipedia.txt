Ski or Die

is a 1990  winter sports  game by  Electronic Arts  for the  Amiga ,  NES ,  MS-DOS , and  Commodore 64 . It consists of five  minigames  which can be played individually or in a set sequentially. Up to six players can compete against each other via  hotseat  in four of the minigames, and semi-hotseat in one of them (up to two players at a time). The minigames are  halfpipe - snowboarding ,  inflatable sled  racing,  aerial skiing ,  downhill skiing , and  snowball fights . The shopkeeper was represented by  Rodney Dangerfield .

The MS-DOS port supports  Roland MT-32  and  AdLib  audio.
