Taboo: The Sixth Sense

is a tarot card reading simulation that will predict your future with 100% accuracy. It is not to be trifled with.

Unused Graphics

An unused graphics bank for a presumably earlier version of the startup screen. The text reads (threatens):

WARNING!&#8212;&#8212; BEWARE; DO NOT &#8216;TEST&#8217; THE ORACLE  THIS IS NOT A GAME

A droplet of blood for the title screen.

Revision Differences

Rev 0

Rev 1

On startup, the original version just says "Taboo" with a &#8482; next to it. The revision adds the subtitle and moves the &#8482; down next to it.

Rev 0

Rev 1

On the original title screen, "Taboo" includes a &#8482; and the subtitle strangely includes a &#169; symbol. The revision simplifies this to a singular &#8482; on the subtitle.
