The NES

Total Recall

is a pretty terrible adaptation of the 1990 sci-fi movie of the same name.

Unused Music

A cover of the main theme from the original movie. Apparently, Acclaim didn't have the rights to use said music, hence it goes unused.

On a side note, George Sanger, the music composer, recalls making tracks for the game that never made it into the final product.

(Source:  Video Game Music Preservation Foundation )

Partially Unseen Sprite

In level 1, cars speed across the street at the bottom of the screen, shooting bullets. Normally in the North American version, the lowest part of the sprite is never shown, rendered off-screen. However if playing the European version of the game, or if the resolution is expanded, it can be seen in full. It also looks much better against a dark background, unlike the lighter grey of the road.
