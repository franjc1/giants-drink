Super Bases Loaded

is a  baseball  video game produced by  Jaleco  for the  Super Nintendo Entertainment System  in 1991. It is the fifth overall installment of the  Bases Loaded  series, and first installment of the secondary series for the Super NES. This game was originally released in Japan under the title

Super Professional Baseball

(  スーパープロフェッショナルベースボール  ) .

&#91; 3 &#93;

The North American version includes a sponsorship from  Ryne Sandberg , like  Bases Loaded 3  for the NES.
