Unused Graphics

A small Coca-Cola logo is present in the ROM at offset

F08B

(Revision 0)/  F18B

(Revision 1). It would appear that during the game's development, Coca-Cola wanted to advertise its ownership of Merv Griffin Enterprises (the company that created and produced  Jeopardy! ), but the sale of MGE to Columbia Pictures Entertainment in 1988 scuttled the idea.

Likely due to sharing many other assets,  Junior Edition  contains the logo at offset

F434  . In  25th Anniversary Edition , which also shares many assets, only five tiles of the logo remain at offset

ECF3  .

A similar instance of the Coca-Cola logo is  present  (and also unused) in the NES  Wheel of Fortune .
