The SNES version of

The Jungle Book

is essentially a completely different game compared other versions, involving more linear (but still exploration-heavy) levels where you must find either Baloo or an exit sign (unless there's a boss of course) as opposed to collecting a set amount of gems first.

Extra Options

While the Virgin logo is on the screen, press  Up, Up, Up, B, B, Y, Y, Select  and listen for a sound. Then, press  Up, Down, Left, Right, B, Up, Y, Y  and listen for another sound. Aside from a Level Select, there will also be "Cheat" in the Options menu. This can be set up to 5, and with each number, another cheat will be activated, which are as follows:

1  - Press  L  to float up.

2  - Unknown.

3  - Have 88 of each weapon.

4  - Press  R  for frame advance.

5  - Unknown.
