Wayne's World

on the SNES relives the greatest moments from the film, such as the time when Wayne fought a fat robotic Elvis with his supersonic guitar.

Test Dialogue

Pro Action Replay  (PAR) code

06F35B81 06F35CF6

will replace all dialogue with this placeholder text.

These graphics are loaded into VRAM upon loading most stages and are quickly overwritten by the player graphics. They list the enemies in the stage.

Level Select

When Wayne and Garth say "Wayne's World, Wayne's World, Party Time, Excellent", press  X + L + R  and  Up + Y + B . Press  Select  to choose a level and  Start  to play it.
