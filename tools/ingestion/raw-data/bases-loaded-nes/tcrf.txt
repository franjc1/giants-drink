This game has  regional differences .

Originally released as

Moero!! Pro Yakyuu

for the Famicom,

Bases Loaded

for the NES is the first game in the  Moero!!  and  Bases Loaded  series respectively.

Regional Differences

Title Screen

The logo was completely changed, the player graphic was changed, the baseball was redrawn, and the copyright information has been updated.

Japan

USA

Miscellanous

The letters of the teams were changed. Also the way "SELECT YOUR TEAM" displays was changed.

Japan

USA

The Japanese version has animated flags while the USA version does not.

In addition, the names on the scoreboard were changed; Tanaka (タナカ), Hayashi (ハヤシ), Murata (ムラタ), and Ohta (オオタ). All four names belong to staff of the game's developer, TOSE; Tanaka is Tetsuji Tanaka, the game's graphic artist. Hayashi and Murata are the game's sound designers Akihito Hayashi and Kouji Murata, respectively. Ohta is Nobukazu Ohta, the programmer.
