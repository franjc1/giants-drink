Before both characters would appear later in  Kirby  series as Lololo and Lalala, there was

Adventures of Lolo

by the same developer.

While it is the 6th game in the  Eggerland  series, it marks the very first game to be released in North America, as previous titles were released only in Japan while the first two MSX games saw releases in Europe, it contains levels from its previous games, with most of them being taken from  Eggerland: Souzou e no Tabidachi  while some of them are taken from

Eggerland: Meikyuu no Fukkatsu

and  Eggerland Mystery , and the final stage in this game was edited from  Eggerland 2 . It never released in Japan, but the graphic overhaul in this game would be used in later sequels, Lolo would also become HAL America's mascot, seen in some of their advertisement flyers.
