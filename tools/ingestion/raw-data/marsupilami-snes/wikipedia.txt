Marsupilami

is a video game developed by British studio Apache Software and published by  Sega  for the  Genesis  in 1995–1996.

The game received average reviews.

Next Generation

said, "all in all,  Marsupilami  is a traditional side-scrolling platform game with the gameplay aimed toward a younger audience. If you're searching for the same old gameplay,  Marsupilami  is for you."

&#91; 9 &#93;

Spanish magazine

HobbyConsolas  , however, gave it 82%.

&#91; 6 &#93;

GamePro

summed up the review by saying, " Marsupilami  is packaged as a kid's game, but the stiff controls and quick timer make the puzzle solving tough and may drive younger gamers to tears. The perky, comical animations are pleasant enough, but the plucky, happy circus music grates. In short, keep this cart confined at your nearest retailer."
