Batman: The Video Game

is a side-scrolling platformer based on Tim Burton's 1989  Batman  movie.

Sub-Page

Prototype Info

Unused Item

This item is laying around in the first stage of  the prototype , and nowhere else. It can be picked up and, as you may have guessed, awards an extra life. Only its graphics and tilemap remain in the final, which is kind of odd considering how little code it must have required.

Regional Differences

The BGM of Stages 1 and 2 are swapped in the Japanese version.

The boss of Stage 1 has twice the health in the Japanese version (like in the prototype).
