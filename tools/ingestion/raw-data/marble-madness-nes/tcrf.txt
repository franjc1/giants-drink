Unused Text

This copyright is sprinkled throughout the US version ROM, first occurring at

0x8  :

(C) COPYRIGHT 1988 RARE LTD. ENGLAND.

At

0x7F90

and many other places:

PAUL.P & RACHEL.M.E

The same names or initials can be found in other games by the same developing team from Rare:

California Games

and

Wizards & Warriors  , as well as the same names often mentioned in the default records table for these games and in the

Wheel of Fortune

series.

The initials stand for Paul Proctor and Rachel M. Edwards, the game developers.

In the European version, only this string exists:

(C) COPYRIGHT 1990 RARE LTD.

Regional Differences

US

Europe

The default high score table names are different between the European and American releases, with Simon and Paul being the only two present in both.
