Censorship

Just like the SNES port of Fatal Fury 2, the international versions of Special edit a few details of two characters:

Mai's thong was made bigger and her breasts don't bounce in her "Nippon Ichi!" victory pose.

Laurence Blood's last name is shortened to just "B.". His "Bloody Spin" voice clip was kept this time though.

Playable Characters

Japan/US

Europe

The Japanese and US versions were released on 32 megabit cartridges and feature all the 15 playable characters plus Ryo Sakazaki from the  Art of Fighting  series, who is playable via a cheat code. The PAL version, however, was released on a 24 megabit cartridge; as a result, Big Bear, Cheng Sinzan, Axel Hawk, and Laurence Blood had to be removed along with their respective stages, with Ryo being playable from the start in exchange.

(Source:  Rage Quitter 87 )
