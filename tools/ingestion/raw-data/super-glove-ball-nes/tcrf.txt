This game has a  hidden level select .

Super Glove Ball

is one of only two games in the "Power Glove Gaming Series", the other game being  Bad Street Brawler .

Level Select

At the title screen, press  Up, Left, Down, A, Right, A, B, A  on a standard controller or  6, 5, 4, 7, 4, 0, 7  on the Power Glove to add a level select to the bottom of the screen. Press  Select  to choose a level.
