The SNES

Batman Forever

is a mediocre

Mortal Kombat  -esque beat-'em-up with awkward controls. Too bad you can't do fatalities.

Level Select

On the main menu, press  Left, Up, Left, Left, A, B, Y  (LULLABY). The screen will flash to indicate success. Change the game type to Normal Game and choose your character to bring up the level select screen. Pressing  Up  or  Down  will toggle the stage, and  Left  or  Right  will toggle the level. In a level, pressing  Start, L, R  will inflict damage on all enemies on screen.
