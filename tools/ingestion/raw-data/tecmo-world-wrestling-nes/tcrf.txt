Sound Test

Hold  Up + Left + A + B  at the title screen, then press  Select . Use  Up / Down  to select the music, and press  B  to play it.

Staff Roll

The Japanese version has a completely different staff roll where, on top of using aliases, each developer has their own quotation as well.

The US version's staff roll has a slightly different order than the Japanese version, so it's been reorganized to follow the latter's order for comparison. The Japanese version also has an extra screen for graphic artists.
