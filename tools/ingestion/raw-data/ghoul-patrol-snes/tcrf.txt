Ghoul Patrol

is the consequence of developing a game never intended to be a sequel to

Zombies Ate My Neighbors  , with very little involvement from the original team, but shoehorning in the ZAMN license and characters half-way through in a shallow attempt to gain extra sales.

A Genesis port was in development in tandem with the SNES version, but was dropped in order to focus on the latter.
