Baseball

is an early sports game for the Famicom, one of the NES' launch titles in North America, and one of the FDS' launch titles.

Title Screen

Japan

International

The copyright was changed to mention Nintendo of America and strangely omits the symbol in the process. Note that the American copyright attributes the year to 1984; this most certainly coincides with Nintendo's early, failed plans to launch the NES in the states.

Playable Teams

The international versions changed the letter choices for the various teams. Notably, while no version had a license to use official team names, they nevertheless feature the team name initials of NPB Central League or American MLB teams respectively.
