Donkey Kong 3

is the NES port of the black sheep of the  Donkey Kong  trilogy.

Unused Sprites

Tiles for Donkey Kong's hands that don't have vines overlapping them. Most of these tiles could have been used for when DK is at the bottom of the vines, but they never are.

Revisional Differences

In the

Animal Crossing

release, the controls were slightly modified so the player can press  Up  and  Down  to switch between options at the title screen.
