Clu Clu Land

is a game where you play as Bubbles as she clings her way around an enclosed arena, avoiding spikey things and warpholes to reveal the outline/shape of some thing, like a pineapple.

Version Differences

Animal Crossing

The version present in

Animal Crossing

has a few modifications (see notes page for details):

The  3,000-point bonus bug  was fixed.

The PRG-ROM size was expanded from 16KiB to 32KiB (as seen in

Donkey Kong  ) in order to insert new delay code before the idle loop. This may have been done to work around emulator timing issues.

Nintendo Switch Online

The version present in  Nintendo Entertainment System - Nintendo Switch Online  slows down the flashing effect that occurs when clearing a stage.
