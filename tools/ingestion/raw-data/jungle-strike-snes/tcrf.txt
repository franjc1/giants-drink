Hidden Messages

Jungle Strike  features four special passwords which display messages from the developers when entered. One of them even doubles as the (former) phone number of programmer Richard Stevenson, with an invitation to give him a call.

T0M#N#J3RRY#

0114#2475549  (that's +44 (114) 247 5549 for our non-UK readers)

SS#F0R#MLVC#

SH3FF13LD###

(Source: Original TCRF research)
