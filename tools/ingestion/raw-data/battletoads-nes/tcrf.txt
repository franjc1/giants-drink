Battletoads

is a super-challenging action game with various styles of gameplay (fighting, platforming, racing, etc.) and excellent graphics. Oh, and there's a massive bug in two-player mode.

Both this game and its  arcade sequel  were resurrected in  Rare Replay  for the Xbox One, nearly 25 years later.

Unused Music

Track #8 on the NSF. Unknown purpose, though probably for Level 5 (Surf City) or Level 9 (Terra Tubes) since they're the only two stages that share background music.

(Source: FlyingOmelette)

While not entirely unused, you normally only hear about half (or less) of the post-level theme during normal gameplay. The second half of the song is not present in any other version of the game.

Revisional Differences

In late 2021, a Japanese company named Columbus Circle re-released the Japanese version of  Battletoads . Only a couple of things were changed, however.
