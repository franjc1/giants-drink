Several Software Creations games (including this,

Target Renegade  ,

Magic Johnson's Fast Break  ,

Pictionary  ,

Terminator 2: Judgment Day  , and

Treasure Master  ) had their empty space padded with copyright/credits text, rather than

00

or

FF

as most other games did.

Sky Shark  contains the following text:

SKY SHARK

COPYRIGHT TAITO AMERICA CORP

PROGRAMMER

STEPHEN RUDDY 1988

ARTIST

ANDREW THRELFALL

MUSICIAN

TIM FOLLIN

SOFTWARE CREATIONS LTD
FIRST FLOOR

204 OXFORD ROAD
MANCHESTER

M1 5QA
GREAT BRITAIN
TEL 061 236 1154
FAX 061 236 8248

JELLYFISH BEANS AND CUSTARD

Note that there are no actual line feed symbols in the text; each line is a fixed length, padded with spaces.
