Stage Select

Starting at SNES address

$808613

is programming for a Stage Select menu. These game enhancer codes are needed to access it:

Game Genie

Pro Action Replay  (PAR)

C16B-14AF

C96D-CFAF

CEC8-376F

C2E8-1078

80859BA6

808607A5

80A7BEAF

9EFDB0AD

After entering one of the above code sets, begin a new game. When the cutscene of the night sky appears, press  Start .

In the Stage Select menu, press  Up  or  Right  to activate the Stage Select text and increase the Stage and Area values.  A ,  B ,  X , and  Y  can also be used to increase and decrease the values. Press  Start  when finished, then turn the codes off.

(Source: gedowski, JLukas)

Regional Differences

To do:  There may be more changes, especially in the cutscenes.

Both the Japanese and western versions of the game are presented in English, but some text was touched up between releases.

Japanese

English

Tall Crown

Tall Clown

B. Test Tube

Test Tube

Additionally, screens were added after the credits in the western versions telling the player to try a higher difficulty, with a special 'You are the True Dark Knight' message for completing the game on Mania. The Japanese version freezes at the end of the credits with no such screens.

Catwoman

During the first battle against Catwoman, there are two different scenes between the Japanese and western versions after defeating her. The western version shows the "Scene Clear" title first with the later transition of Catwoman getting up and deliberately jumping off the building; in the Japanese version, this transition is backwards. An intriguing melody that would be used after her second battle's defeat is used here first with an animation of a weakened Catwoman getting up until she falls off the building with the "Score Clear" title appearing later on.

After this, it follows the cutscenes where she's "saved by kitty-litter" in the same for both versions.
