Stack-Up

is the second entry in the two-game Robot Series (designed to be used with R.O.B., the first being

Gyromite  ). While  Gyromite  could be bought by itself or in the NES Deluxe Set,  Stack-Up  was only ever sold by itself, which makes complete copies quite rare.

Bizarrely, like  Gyromite , most  Stack-Up  cartridges merely contained a copy of the Famicom's  Robot Block  with an adapter so it could work on the NES.
