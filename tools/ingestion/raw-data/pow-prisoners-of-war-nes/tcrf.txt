P.O.W.: Prisoners of War

is a port of the  arcade beat 'em up  of the same name.

Level Select

The  Game Genie  codes

IPKGAKIY + XTNKGTAV

re-enable an in-game level select. To use it, press  A  on the second controller to toggle level select mode "on" or "off" (a number will appear at the bottom-right corner of the game HUD) and press  Right  to cycle through level numbers 1 to 5. Then press  B  to advance to the selected level.
