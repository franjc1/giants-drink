The NES

Klax

is another of Tengen's ports of Atari arcade games to Nintendo's console. Similar to

Super Sprint  , the Japanese Famicom version was actually licensed by Nintendo.

Bit Reversal

This game's samples are corrupted by the 2A03's bit reversal bug. For more information, click  here .

Regional Differences

Title Screen

The title screen has a couple minor differences. Interestingly, unlike  Super Sprint , they left the staff roll in the Japanese version.

US/KR

JP

The logo was changed to match the new publisher. Bizarrely, the Japanese version doesn't use Hudson's traditional logo, but instead writes it in the Tengen logo's font. The copyright text was also changed.

Level Select

US/KR

JP

The Japanese version changes the button to start as Player 2 from  Start  to  A , since the Famicom's second controller lacks the former.
