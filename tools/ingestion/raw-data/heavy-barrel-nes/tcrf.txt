Heavy Barrel

is a port of the arcade shoot-em-up.

Level Select

In the Japanese version at the title screen, hold the following buttons on controller 2 then press  Start  on controller 1:

Up + Right  - Level 2

Right  - Level 3

Down + Right  - Level 4

Down  - Level 5

Down + Left  - Level 6

Left  - Level 7

(Source: cah4e3)
