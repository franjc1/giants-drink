MIDI Data

To do:  MIDI chunks are also present in the Genesis/MD and Game Boy versions of the game.

There are eight chunks of MIDI data hidden in the ROM. They can be reproduced with VLC (with the FluidSynth soundfont), but since we are missing the original instruments used in the data, all you will hear is the player's default piano sounds. The chunks themselves contain some instruments names though.
