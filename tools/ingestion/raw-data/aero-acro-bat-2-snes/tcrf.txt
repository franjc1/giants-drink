Level Select

In the Sound Test, play sounds  10, 15, 06, 08 . Now start the game, pause, and press  Down + A + L + R .

Unused Text

Located at $05CB70 are these unused bits of text.

LIVES ON LIVES OFF

ENERGY ON ENERGY OFF

These  are  used in  the Genesis version  when you enable the "Infinite Energy and Lives" cheat, which the SNES version does not have, nor is there leftover code for activating it.

Regional Differences

Please elaborate.

Having more detail is always a good thing.

In PAL only, one level is unbeatable by normal means due to an oversight believed to be tied to framerate and the cannon. Have to use the hidden level select to move on. NTSC does not have this issue.
