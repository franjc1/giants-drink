It is important to note that in the level select for the Sega Genesis release, "Cathedral" was changed to "See Jim Run", a still early but more close to final name for See Jim Run, Run Jim Run. Additionally, Villi People is called "Jim Is Now A Blind Cave Salamander!" in the Genesis release, however, the cheat menu was not updated to reflect this and still says "Villi People" on that version of the game.

Broken Cheat Code

Pause the game and press  A, B, X, Select, Select, X, B, A . Once entered, the level will restart showing no level name, causing the game to lock up. If you quickly press  Start  when the screen appears, it's possible to skip to the main menu, though this causes graphical glitches.

Its purpose may have been "Suicide", a cheat code present in the PlayStation and Saturn versions.

Television Item

US

EU

A rare item found in a few levels changes the palette of everything except for sprites to grayscale. Collecting this on ISO 9000 changes the dull environment to a more colorful one instead. Grabbing another TV in the same level or traveling to a new area reverts the colors back to normal.

It is noted that the TV item is mentioned in the US manual but does not appear in any level.

Playmates Interactive Logo

US

EU

The European release adds a wave effect on the logo's reflection.

Lorenzo's Soil

Pressing  Up  +  X  to deploy the Manta Shield right before the timer hits zero and holding on while the rocks fall deals no damage to Jim. While this is also possible in the US version, it is far less consistent and often damages Jim.

Villi People

US

EU

Getting near the villi distorts the entire level at different speeds depending on the proximity like in the Genesis version. The US release only speeds up the villi.

Flyin' King

US

EU

The game loads the lake graphics much faster in the European release.
