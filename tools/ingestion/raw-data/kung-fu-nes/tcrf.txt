Kung Fu

is a classic port of the  arcade game . It's notable for being an obscenely rare (if not the only) instance of Nintendo porting an arcade game they did not develop.

Version Differences

In Japan, the game was released for the Famicom as  Spartan X , named after  the film of the same name  (though the story is much more in line with  Game of Death ). When Nintendo lost the  Spartan X  license in Japan, the Famicom game was re-released as  Kung Fu ; this version is identical to its NES counterpart.  &#91;5&#93;

Spartan X

Kung Fu

The logo was changed, the font color was changed from orange to blue, and two dragons were added.
