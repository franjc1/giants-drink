Based on the Disney movie and being more of a port of the  Genesis game  than other versions,

The Jungle Book

on NES is perhaps most well-known for being the basis of an infamous pirated hack based on  The Lion King  featuring a twerking Simba.

Hidden Menu

At the sound test, play sounds  40, 30, 20, 19, 18, 17, 16, 15 . Pressing  Left / Right  will set the Level Select. The other options are as follows:

Solid Bottom Mode  - Closes off the bottom edge of a level so it can't be fallen through.

No Energy Loss  - Infinite energy.

Infinite Lives

Infinite Time

Single Gem Mode  - Only one gem required to complete each level.

Force Bonus Level  - Play bonus level after each stage without having to find the Shovel.

(Source: GameFAQs (Dallas))
