Unused Graphics

The Old Tree Boss

The boss only ever looks down and to either side, rendering this frame unused.

This little red hopping bird and egg are loaded with the boss graphics, but are never used. In the later Genesis port, the boss drops an egg whenever it pauses and squawks, though it never hatches into a bird.

(Source: Rachel Mae)

Unused Music

Two of the game's music tracks have unused echo variations. Use the  Pro Action Replay  codes

00817D?? 0094DD8D

(where "??" is the song ID) to play them at the title and password screens.
