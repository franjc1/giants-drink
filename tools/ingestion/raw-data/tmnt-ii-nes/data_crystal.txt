Miscellaneous

This game was based on the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles arcade game. Some of the stages were changed, but the scoring system was not.

The NES version had a limited number of continues. (By contrast, the arcade version could be played continuously, as long as the player had quarters remaining.)
