Beavis and Butt-Head

is an arcade style beat-'em-up game based on the show that actually uses the license well! Imagine that.

This game has been positively received, save for one fatal glitch with the password system where you don't get any more lives and your health is almost gone. This leads you to die pretty quickly before you even start the game.

Unused Graphics

By removing the bg 1 with an emulator,you can see that behind the black bar with the dialogues of the two characters, there's a hidden part of the background with the TV still incomplete, as the blue part that was supposed to be transparent is still visible. You can also see the entire pizza box with a stain on it and some crumbs on the floor.

By removing the 'BG1' from the Beavis and Butthead warning screen, which states they are not role models, you can view the background of 'BG2' from the Highland streets level, although the palettes do not match.
