Developer Message

A message from developers Pierre Adane and Philippe Dessoly can be found right at the beginning of the ROM. The message exists in all regional versions, with both the US and Japanese versions having a build date along with the message. The text does not have any line breaks; they have been added for easier reading:

Europe:

MR NUTZ produced by Pierre ADANE & Philippe DESSOLY. 
If you want to contact us then write to: 
Pierre ADANE - BP 67 - 94242 l'Hay les roses cedex - France -

USA:

NTSC version 11 jan 94 - MR NUTZ produced by Pierre ADANE & 
Philippe DESSOLY. If you want to contact us then write to:

Pierre ADANE - BP 67 - 94242 l'Hay les roses cedex - France -

Japan:

NTSC - Version 20 june 1994 - MR NUTZ produced by Pierre ADANE & 
Philippe DESSOLY. If you want to contact us then write to: 
Pierre ADANE - BP 67 - 94242 l'Hay les roses cedex - France -

Level Skip

On the title screen, hold  Start+Select+L+R  and then press  Up, Up, Up, Down, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right  on controller 2. The word "Start" will change to "Cheater" upon correct code entry. Now during gameplay, press  R  on controller one to skip to the next level.
