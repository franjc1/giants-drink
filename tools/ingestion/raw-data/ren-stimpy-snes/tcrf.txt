Level Select

At the title screen, press  L, R, A, L, R, L, B, R, X . A

01

will appear underneath the title, denoting the first level of the game. Press  Select  to change the level number, and  Start  to go to the selected level.

Unused Graphics

During the demo mode or loading levels, when the player sprites are not yet visible, they are replaced by a rectangle that says 'Ren/Stimpy'. This rectangle is used as a placeholder to indicate that Ren or Stimpy's sprites are loading.The rectangle doesn't have a specific palette, but it appears to use the one from Ren or Stimpy's sprites.If you stop the emulation, for example with Mesen, and check the OAM1 section, you will be able to see the rectangle in place of the player sprites.
