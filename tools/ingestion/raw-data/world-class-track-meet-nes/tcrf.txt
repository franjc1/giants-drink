Stadium Events

is one of only two games in the Family Trainer series (the other being  Athletic World ) that were released in North America. Nintendo purchased the North American rights to the games and the Family Fun Fitness mat (which was rebranded as the Power Pad) in 1988, and both games were immediately recalled.

Although only about 200 copies of the US  Stadium Events  are believed to exist, the game can still be found rather easily under its much more well-known title  World Class Track Meet .

Version Differences

Japan

US Original

Europe

US Rerelease

In addition to the logo and text differences,  Running Stadium  has a dark blue sky whereas  Stadium Events  has a light blue one. The Japanese flags were also replaced with US ones, the colors of the stadium background were changed and the whole thing was moved 2 tiles up to accommodate the longer copyright text.

While  Stadium Events  uses a logo in the same style as the one in  Running Stadium ,  World Class Track Meet  uses a completely different logo, along with darkening the background color (similar to that of the original game, but a bit less saturated), recoloring the option text from dark blue to white, changing the stadium colors yet again, and moving everything down 1 tile to accommodate the  shorter  copyright text. The "Mat B" graphic was also changed to match the mat's rebranding.

US/Europe

The Tournament opponent swapped species, and some awkward English was cleaned up, alongside the text being moved down.
